⚠️ Minecraft Server Rules ⚠️

🔒 Necessary Rules

🚫 No PvP unless both players agree.
🚫 Do not destroy buildings unless they are yours.
🚫 Do not destroy villages.
🚫 Do not place unnecessary or random blocks.
🚫 Do not go to the End or kill the Ender Dragon without admin permission.
🚫 No abusing or offensive language.
🚫 No griefing: do not damage or steal other players' creations.
🚫 No hacking or cheating (e.g., X-ray, kill aura, etc.).
🚫 No duplication glitches or exploits.
🚫 No lag machines or excessive auto-farms.
🚫 No spamming chat, commands, or sounds.
🚫 No impersonating staff members.
🚫 Respect protected areas and claims.
🚫 Follow staff instructions — their decisions are final.

⚙️ Optional Rules

📅 Be respectful and friendly to others.
📅 Use appropriate language in public chat.
📅 Prefer English in public chat for moderation.
📅 No advertising other servers or Discords.
📅 No AFK machines or auto-clickers.
📅 Don’t scam or trick other players in trades.
📅 Don’t exploit shop or job systems unfairly.
📅 Bedrock players should use real usernames.
📅 Use /msg and teleport commands responsibly.
